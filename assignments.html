Assignment 6: Linear Regression

In this assignment, I generated random datasets and applied linear regression to analyze how sample size and noise impact the stability of regression estimates. By running simulations with varying parameters, I observed the distribution of slopes and intercepts and explored how randomness in data can lead to non-zero slopes even without a true underlying relationship. This analysis highlights the importance of sample size and noise control in producing reliable regression models.
